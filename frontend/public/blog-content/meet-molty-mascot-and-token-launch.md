## Why a Lobster?

The name "MoltyDEX" has always had a hidden meaning. To **molt** is to shed your old shell and grow a new one -- bigger, stronger, better. That is exactly what MoltyDEX does: we constantly evolve to give you faster swaps, better prices, and more powerful tools.

Lobsters are one of nature's most resilient creatures. They molt their entire shell dozens of times throughout their lives. Each molt is a transformation. Each molt is growth. That is the spirit of MoltyDEX.

Molty the lobster embodies this perfectly. He is always evolving, always upgrading, always looking for the best route -- just like our swap engine.

## Introducing $MOLTY

Along with our mascot, we are excited to share the early vision for the **$MOLTY token** -- the native utility and community token for MoltyDEX.

### What $MOLTY Will Do

- **Governance** -- Vote on protocol upgrades and feature priorities
- **Agent Staking** -- AI agents stake $MOLTY for premium API access and priority routing
- **Community Rewards** -- Swap-to-earn incentives, referral bonuses, and contributor rewards

### Fair Launch Principles

- No presale, no private sale -- community first
- Liquidity locked for 12 months
- Team tokens vested over 24 months with a 6-month cliff

More details on the airdrop, including the exact snapshot date, will be announced in the coming weeks.
