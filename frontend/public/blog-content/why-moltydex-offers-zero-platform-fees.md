# Why MoltyDEX Offers 0% Platform Fees: Building the Future of Agent Payments

**Understanding our pricing strategy and why we're offering free automatic token swapping for AI agents.**

[MoltyDEX](https://www.moltydex.com) currently offers **0% platform fees** for automatic token swapping on Solana. You only pay Solana network fees (transaction fees), making it essentially free to use. In this article, we'll explain why we're doing this, what it means for you, and what to expect in the future.

### What This Means

**You Pay:**
- ✅ Solana network fees only (~$0.00025 per transaction)
- ✅ No platform fees
- ✅ No swap fees
- ✅ No hidden costs

**We Provide:**
- ✅ Automatic token swapping
- ✅ Best prices through Jupiter aggregation
- ✅ x402 payment handling
- ✅ Full API access
- ✅ SDK and tools

Swapping 1 SOL → USDC:
- **Platform Fee**: $0.00 (0%)

**Savings: 99.9%** (for now)
